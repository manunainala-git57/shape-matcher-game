How to Play

  • This is a shape matcher game where shapes will fall down. Drag and drop them into the suitable shape boxes.
  • Correct matches will add 10 points to your score.
  • Incorrect matches will deduct 5 points from your score.
  • If a shape touches the border without being matched, 1 point will be deducted.
  • If your score goes below 0 , the game will end. You can replay it afterward.
